What Exactly Is the Unsent Project?

A massive digital collection of anonymous messages written to past lovers, ex-best friends, crushes, and people who left an emotional mark.

The Psychology Behind Unsent Messages

Why do people send messages into the void instead of confronting someone directly?

Psychologists often describe unsent messages as a form of emotional catharsis. When you type your truth but don't send it, you still lighten the emotional load.

The Unsent Project uniquely merges emotion, aesthetics, and storytelling, making it psychologically comforting and artistically appealing.
